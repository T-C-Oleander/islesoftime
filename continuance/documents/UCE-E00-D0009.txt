NOTE ON THIS FILING:

This is the final document filed by the UCE Founding Assembly as a unit.
What follows is not law. It is not governance. It is the record of what
the Assembly understood when the founding act was complete.

The Assembly will continue to meet. The UCE Assembly will govern.
The founding act is over.

================================================================================
[ WHAT WAS BUILT ]
================================================================================

Nine documents.

A charter that states the animating principle without qualification.

A ratification of the Failsafe Protocol -- the instrument this
Assembly did not write but chose to be bound by, because what it
requires is exactly what this Assembly requires of itself.

A doctrine for the Citadels -- the physical answer to the species'
tendency to consume itself. Built not from faith in human goodness
but from the calculation that engineering survival is possible
and leaving it to chance is not.

A defensive architecture doctrine that does not ask adversaries
to be peaceful. It asks them to be capable of arithmetic.

Economic accords that establish the floor and hold it. That do not
pretend the floor is the ceiling. That hold the floor anyway.

Three pillars designated -- not created, because two of them predate
this Assembly -- formally recognized as carrying what governance
cannot carry when governance is the problem.

A doctrine for the containment zones that files, in its first line,
the thing this Assembly did not want to file:
The Citadels cannot hold everyone.

An administrative framework that describes how principles become
decisions and how decisions reach the ground.

This is what was built.

================================================================================
[ WHAT IT COST ]
================================================================================

Four billion people died in the wars.

The Assembly does not have four billion names. The record has
recovered what it could. There are gaps the record will not fill --
not for want of trying, but because the evidence is gone.

What the Assembly can file:

The cost of the Citadels was paid by people who did not choose
to pay it. The infrastructure exists because labor was not always
compensated at its true value. The stability exists because the
immediate post-war period required decisions that were not all made
with full consent. The classified instrument that preceded the DCCZ
framework is in the archive under restricted classification. It is
there because this Assembly chose to record what it did rather than
pretend it was not done.

The cost is in the record.
It will remain in the record.

================================================================================
[ WHAT THE ASSEMBLY COULD NOT FIX ]
================================================================================

The Assembly could not fix human nature. The documents reflect this.

Every provision in the founding series contains the acknowledgment
that it may be honored in form and violated in spirit. The advisory
loop may slow until it becomes procedural. The DCCZ reviews may be
filed without substance. The Entropy Accords floor may be held while
everything above it becomes a mechanism for permanent extraction.
The Three Pillars may drift toward the government they were designated
to remain independent from.

The Assembly knew this when it wrote these documents.
The Assembly wrote them anyway.

Not because the documents prevent failure.
Because they make failure visible.
A failure that is documented can be corrected.
A failure that was never recorded becomes precedent.

The Assembly chose the record over the aspiration.

Preserve.
Record.
Reconcile.
