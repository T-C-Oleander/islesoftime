[ PREAMBLE ]
================================================================================

I started this project a decade ago without realizing it until about a year ago. 
Its goes back farther than that, but that is borderline semantics on "when did 
it actually start". 2015-16. A dnd campaign, hopes and dreams paired with an 
intro to business class in high school. This spawned the whispers of what would 
become DARKWORLD. DARKWORLD went through many iterations, some peaceful, most 
harrowing. But somewhere along the way, it began to actually breathe, evolve 
and even speak to me in a language I didn't think I knew.

Now, DARKWOLRD is still its own universe, but deeply connected to what its 
birthed. Through its ashes came STARPATH. Most of what dark world was, survived. 

With a more hopeful lens. 

But this is turning into a PRERAMBLE.

[ OKAY WHAT IS THIS? ]
================================================================================

This is a fork of my own personal archival document software. In tune with the 
Atlas Directive in STARPATH. 

After the wars, everything needed to be put back together, rediscovered, 
preserved, recorded and reconciled. Everything that could be was put into the 
archive, from physical items in vaults, to digital documentation and code of 
now forgotten companies and institutions. For me, to consolidate in a in 
universe format (At least for STARPATH) all of the Universal history that has 
been compiled by and the atlas directive in universe. A meta archive if you 
will. I built the program the institution in world would be using, as well the 
populace. 

To never again forget its own history, Or itself.
