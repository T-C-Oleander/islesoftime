NOTE ON THIS FILING:

The Entropy Accords were negotiated during the late period of UNE
consolidation, approximately 2087-2107 CE, as the Citadel Project
reached operational scale and the question of economic architecture
became urgent. They represent the first formal agreement between
the UNE and surviving CIN-aligned corporate and administrative
structures on the terms under which economic activity within the
UCE framework is permitted.

The name comes from the drafting council's operating principle:
that an economic system which consumes its own base is not an
economy. It is entropy. The Accords are the document that makes
this principle law.

================================================================================
[ PREAMBLE ]
================================================================================

The Resource Wars did not happen because humanity ran out of
resources. They happened because humanity organized the distribution
of resources in a way that guaranteed conflict over them.

The Accords do not prevent the accumulation of wealth. They do not
prohibit profit. They do not establish equality. The drafting council
examined each of these options and rejected them -- not because they
are undesirable but because they are unenforceable at civilizational
scale, and instruments that cannot be enforced are not instruments.
They are wishes.

What can be enforced -- what this document enforces -- is the floor.
The minimum condition under which the population base that generates
all economic activity can continue to exist and continue to generate it.

Everything above the floor is not the UCE's concern.
The floor is the UCE's concern.
The floor is load-bearing.

================================================================================
[ THE CORE ARGUMENT ]
================================================================================

Extraction without sustainable demand is structurally identical to
consuming the system that generates the returns.

This is not an ethical claim. It is a thermodynamic one. A system
that extracts value from a population base faster than that
population base can recover and sustain itself is not generating
wealth. It is converting the future into present returns and calling
the accounting profit.

The Resource Wars were the correction of an economic system that had
been doing exactly this for decades. Four billion people died to
balance a ledger that should never have been allowed to accumulate
that kind of debt.

The Entropy Accords are the UCE's formal commitment that the ledger
will not be allowed to accumulate that way again. Not because it is
morally wrong -- the record contains ample evidence that moral
arguments do not reliably constrain economic behavior. Because it
destroys the system. Because the record has documented exactly what
that destruction looks like. Because the UCE exists in the aftermath
of it.

================================================================================
[ WHAT THE ACCORDS ESTABLISH ]
================================================================================

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
I.   ECONOMIC ACTIVITY IS LEGITIMATE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The UCE does not treat commerce, profit, or surplus generation as
suspect. Trade is the mechanism by which resources move to where
they are needed. Profit is the mechanism by which the next trade is
funded. Surplus is what makes everything else possible.

The UCE does not govern how much surplus an enterprise may generate.
The UCE governs the conditions under which that generation occurs.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
II.  THE WELFARE FLOOR
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

All economic activity within UCE jurisdiction must sustain the
population base on which that activity depends at or above
minimum viable threshold.

Minimum viable threshold is defined as:

    -- Caloric sufficiency: The population engaged in or
       supporting the economic activity must be able to maintain
       physical function.

    -- Structural shelter: Protection from environmental
       conditions that would degrade capacity.

    -- Medical sufficiency: Access to intervention against
       conditions that would remove the individual from the
       productive population base permanently.

    -- Educational sufficiency: Access to the foundational
       knowledge base required for the individual to participate
       in the economic activity of the next generation.

These thresholds are floors. They are not aspirations. An enterprise
that sustains its workforce at exactly minimum viable threshold is
in compliance. The UCE does not require generosity. The UCE requires
that the engine not consume its own fuel.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
III. THE EXTRACTION PROHIBITION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Economic activity that degrades the population base below minimum
viable threshold is prohibited. This includes:

    -- Labor conditions that result in systemic mortality or
       permanent incapacity beyond actuarial replacement rates.

    -- Resource allocation that produces caloric deficiency
       within the working population while generating surplus
       for external transfer.

    -- Environmental degradation that removes the population
       from viable habitation without provision for relocation
       and reestablishment.

    -- Debt structures that permanently exclude populations
       from economic participation across generations.

The prohibition does not require intent. An enterprise that produces
these outcomes without intending to is still in violation. Intent
is noted in the record. It does not change the outcome.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
IV.  WHAT THE ACCORDS DO NOT ESTABLISH
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The Accords did not create equality. The floor defines survival,
not fairness. The distance between the floor and the ceiling is
not governed by this instrument.

The Accords did not eliminate disparity. Economic outcomes will
vary. Significantly. The UCE has chosen to govern the floor, not
the distribution above it. That choice is in the record. The
arguments against it are in the record. The UCE made the choice
anyway because the alternative produced instruments that could
not be enforced and therefore produced nothing except the
appearance of governance.

The Accords are not a moral framework. They are a functional one.
The UCE does not require economic actors to be just. It requires
them to sustain the conditions under which their activity
remains possible.

================================================================================
[ ON COMPLIANCE AND DEGRADATION ]
================================================================================

The Accords define compliance and they define its limits.

An enterprise that operates at minimum viable threshold while
extracting maximum surplus above that threshold is in technical
compliance. The UCE is aware this happens. The record documents
where it happens and at what scale. The advisory loop has flagged
the pattern. The flag has been reviewed. The pattern is noted
as concerning and within legal parameters simultaneously.

This is the gap between the floor the Accords establish and the
ceiling they do not. The UCE chose to govern one and not the other.
That choice produces exactly this situation -- legal conduct that
the record identifies as precursor behavior for systemic failure.

The record does not look away from this. The record notes that the
Accords were designed to survive contact with human economic behavior,
and that survival required setting the floor where it could be held.
The flag remains open.
The pattern continues.
The record continues.
