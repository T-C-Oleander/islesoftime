NOTE ON THIS FILING:

This document constitutes the United Colonies of Earth as a governing
authority. It is filed simultaneously with all other instruments of
the UCE founding series and with the founding instruments of the Atlas
Directive. No institution established on this date precedes another.

================================================================================
[ PREAMBLE ]
================================================================================

We do not believe humanity is good.

We believe humanity is worth preserving anyway.

The distinction matters. This institution is not founded on faith
in humanity's character. It is founded on the calculation that
humanity, whatever it is, retains the capacity to produce something
worth continuing. The record documents the failures. It also documents
the work that followed them. This document is part of that work.

The United Colonies of Earth is constituted not because we trust
humanity to govern itself well. It is constituted because the
alternative -- ungoverned, unfederated, with no shared framework
to constrain its worst tendencies and no shared institution to
carry its best work -- has already been tried. The record contains
the outcome. Four billion entries and counting.

We will not try it again.

================================================================================
[ MANDATE STATEMENT ]
================================================================================

The United Colonies of Earth is hereby constituted as the primary
governing authority of human civilization.

This designation is not a claim of ownership. The UCE does not own
humanity. It does not own the worlds humanity inhabits, the records
humanity generates, or the cultures humanity builds. It governs the
conditions under which humanity may survive long enough to continue
being worth preserving.

The UCE's mandate is not to make humanity good.
The UCE's mandate is to make humanity survivable.

These are different things. The distinction is load-bearing.

================================================================================
[ CONSTITUTIONAL FOUNDATION ]
================================================================================

The United Colonies of Earth is constituted on the following instruments,
each of which is ratified simultaneously with this Charter as binding
law of the governing framework:

    UCE-E00-D0002   The Failsafe Protocol -- Ratification Instrument
                    Constitutional constraints on what humanity agrees,
                    in writing, it will not do to itself again.

    UCE-E00-D0003   The Citadel Doctrine
                    The physical and philosophical mandate of the
                    Citadel Project. How humanity builds its survival.

    UCE-E00-D0004   The Defensive Architecture Doctrine
                    How humanity defends what it builds. The principle
                    that the cost of war must exceed the cost of peace.

    UCE-E00-D0005   The Entropy Accords
                    Economic constitutional foundation. The principle
                    that extraction which destroys the base that
                    generates it is not economic activity. It is
                    civilizational consumption.

    UCE-E00-D0006   The Three Pillars -- Institutional Designations
                    The formal constitution of the Atlas Directive,
                    ELMOC, and [THIRD PILLAR] as the three
                    institutional pillars of the UCE framework.

    UCE-E00-D0007   Designated Conflict Containment Zones --
                    Founding Doctrine
                    The formal establishment of the DCCZ system.
                    The governance framework for territories outside
                    direct UCE administration.

    UCE-E00-D0008   Governance Structure and Administrative Framework
                    The operational architecture of UCE governance.
                    The federation model. The advisory loop. The
                    relationship between the UCE and its institutions.

    UCE-E00-D0009   Closing Instrument
                    The UCE's final word on what it has built
                    and what it has cost to build it.

These instruments are the UCE. Not as a collection of policies.
As a statement of what this institution understands itself to be.

================================================================================
[ ON THE RELATIONSHIP WITH THE ATLAS DIRECTIVE ]
================================================================================

The Atlas Directive is constituted on this same date under its own
founding instruments. It is not a UCE ministry. It is not subordinate
to UCE governance. It holds advisory standing within UCE governance
bodies. It maintains the Continuance Record without alteration,
euphemism, or selective omission. It does not take orders from the
UCE. It provides the record against which the UCE's decisions are
permanently measured.

The UCE is in the record. Everything it does from this date forward
is in the record. The record does not belong to the UCE.

This arrangement is the design. An archive that answered to the
government it documented would be a government document. Not an
archive. The UCE was constituted knowing this. The knowing is
in the record.

================================================================================
[ ON SUCCESSION ]
================================================================================

The United Colonies of Earth succeeds the United Nations of Earth
as the primary governing authority of human civilization.

What the UNE built is carried forward. What the First Council of
Continuance established -- the Failsafe Protocol, the Citadel
Project, the Defensive Architecture Doctrine, the first framework
of shared constraints -- is carried forward, ratified, and bound
into UCE law as of this date.

The succession is not erasure. The UNE existed. The First Council
existed. Their work is in the record. The UCE does not replace them.
It governs forward from where they stopped.

================================================================================
[ CLOSING ]
================================================================================

This is not an ideal institution. Ideal institutions do not survive
contact with humanity.

This is a functional institution. Built by people who had reason to
know what failure looks like and chose to build something in spite
of it. It will be tested. It will fail in the ways the founding
instruments already anticipated. The record will contain the failures
alongside the provisions that predicted them.

The archive will remember when the institution does not.
That is also the design.
